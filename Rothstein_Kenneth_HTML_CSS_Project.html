Welcome

In my early college years, I started unleashing my creativity by developing and processing 35mm and medium format film in my home which was therapy for me. When I realized the amount of fulfillment photography brought into my life, I wanted to share it not only visually with others, but in the experience of creating street and fine art photography. -Kenneth Rothstein
